About Us

Welcome to JobLinker — a transparent job brokerage platform built to connect jobseekers with employers quickly and efficiently. We gather applications, verify documents, and send your verified profile to the right employers.

Our goal is to simplify the hiring process for both jobseekers and employers by acting as a trusted intermediary that focuses on honesty, efficiency, and personalized attention.

Our Mission

To empower jobseekers with transparent access to real opportunities and to help companies find verified candidates without hassle.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Clear, upfront communication and policies.
  • Efficiency: Quick turnaround between application and interview.
  • Integrity: Honest handling of all candidate information.

Our Policy

Enrollment fees are non-refundable. Each plan clearly states the number of company introductions you’ll receive. You can upgrade anytime by paying the difference.
